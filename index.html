Gotham Knight's World

Batman is one of the world's most recognizable fictional characters. In the name of his murdered parents, who were taken from him when he was a child, Batman has dedicated his life to an endless crusade, waging war on all criminals. Since that fateful night, he has trained his body and mind to near physical perfection in preparation for becoming a self-made Super Hero. He's amassed a technological arsenal that would put most armies to shame.
